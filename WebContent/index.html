Plant Care

Most species of cactus require minimal care but there are things you can do to keep your cactus extra healthy! Keep your cactus in a window with plenty of sunlight, ideally south facing if possible. Keep the temperature in a range of 19 to 32 degrees celsius during the summer.

Cacti

Did you know that there are about 1750 known cacti species? Cacti have many adaptations to conserve water. Many cacti are succulents which means they have thick fleshy parts designed to store water. Cacti spines are modified leaves which protect against predators and restrict airflow to keep the cactus cool.
